Products Infomation

Unripe plantain flour is a nutrient-dense, gluten-free flour made from dried and finely ground unripe plantains. It is rich in essential nutrients such as dietary fiber, potassium, magnesium, iron, and vitamins A, C, and B-complex. One of its key benefits is its low glycemic index, making it an excellent choice for individuals managing diabetes or seeking to regulate blood sugar levels. Additionally, unripe plantain flour supports digestive health, aids in weight management, and contributes to heart health due to its high fiber content and minimal fat.

This versatile flour is widely used in African and tropical cuisines, particularly for preparing swallow—a staple accompaniment to soups and stews. It can also be used in porridge, pancakes, baked goods, and as a thickening agent in soups and sauces. With its mild, slightly earthy taste, unripe plantain flour seamlessly blends into various recipes, making it a valuable ingredient for those looking to incorporate healthier, natural alternatives into their diet. Its long shelf life and ease of preparation further enhance its appeal as a nutritious and convenient food product.
